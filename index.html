About Tala

Tala Prattoy upholds the art aspects of Durga Puja. Essence of creativity combining the tools and methods of art and art installations interpreting the formative stages of the festival. Tala Prattoy’s work emphasis has always been on Durga Puja as a specific art genre where the public space is being transformed into an art venue. We are committed to make that art global. Durga Puja Art 2024

Tala Prattoy, endeavoring towards global art fervor, projects this year’s Durga Puja Art on celebrating the journey of a Durga Puja Artist. It also highlights Kolkata - narration by Sovanik Paul, the Artist of high repute.

Watch Art with Artist

2024 introduces a new extension to the guided walkthrough to Tala Prattoy Durga Puja Art where visitors can now share one-to-one immersive thoughts through a walk-in schedule with the Artist himself. This program is chargeable and the visitors have to buy tickets for booking the slot at different times.
